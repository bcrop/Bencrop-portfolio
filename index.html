We Love Each Other

Our Story

Let the stigma of online dating be gone. From the ever popular Bumble App, we meet our characters. In fair Santa Barbara, we lay our scene.

  • First Swipe

    November 4, 2019

    In Bumble, the woman initiates the first step. Lucky for both of us, we discovered an instant connection through a love of travel, cycling, camping, and teaching (and our shared love for the Oxford comma). We scheduled our first date soon thereafter.

  • First Date

    November 22, 2019

    Even though Santa Barbara is known for its stunning sunsets, the weather did not agree with Ben's planned beach-picnic outing. Kombuchas instead! A one hour date evolved into an entire evening spent together, filled with meaningful conversation, games, and laughter. A first kiss at the car sealed the deal.

  • In A Relationship

    February 1, 2020

    Amidst one of the most trying two years in our lives, we persevered and built a partnership centered on companionship, love, and adventure. We value our time spent together, and with each other's families in the idyllic Northwest. At home, in the American Riviera, we most enjoy prolonged conversation with close friends, picnics on the beach, and escaping to the top of the mountains by trailer for picturesque sunsets.

  • Engaged

    November 7, 2021

    After three months of too many jewelers and a superb custom ring design, Ben took Johanna up to the top of Camino Cielo - the road that traverses Santa Barbara's mountain range - for an evening together to "look at the stars". In between staged photos, he took a knee and asked Johanna to marry him. With a resounding scream, she said "yes".

    We can't be happier to begin spending the rest of our lives together.
